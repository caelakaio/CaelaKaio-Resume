Caela Kaio is an associate of The Law Offices of Fia and Poko. Her practice is located in Honolulu, Hawaii and is well known throughtout the South Pacific. Her practice focuses on complex civil litigation, government investigations and professional responsibility within the Polynesian Islands.

A cum laude graduate of Richardson School of Law in Honolulu, Hawaii, Caela has clerked twice for the Department of Justice and the Office of International Affairs. She also supported the Hawaii Women's Rights Clinic where she drafted and developed a brief for the Hawaii High Court.

Prior to joining the firm, Caela acted as in house counsel and contract manager for a Fortune 500 company where she counseled on Native Hawaiian rights and international affairs into Hawaii. She represented the company in drafting complex customer agreements with international corporations.
